About Me

Hi! I'm Trung Dong, a first-year PhD student in Electrical & Computer Engineering at the University of South Florida, where I am advised by Prof. Xiaomin Lin. My research focuses on 3D computer vision, multimodal perception, and robust AI for underwater robotics and autonomous systems.

I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of South Florida, where I was advised by Prof. Arman Sargolzaei. During my undergraduate studies, I worked on research in autonomous vehicles, 3D perception, and multimodal fusion, which shaped my current interests in perception and intelligent robotic systems.
